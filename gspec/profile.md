# Puff Paradise — Business Profile

## Overview
Puff Paradise is a smoke and vape shop located in Forney, Texas, serving the local community and surrounding areas. Positioned as a go-to destination for vape enthusiasts and smoke shop customers, Puff Paradise offers a wide selection of popular brands and products, emphasizing quality and variety. The business aims to create a welcoming atmosphere with "great vibes" and an enjoyable shopping experience for its customers.

The shop is known for keeping "the hottest flavors in stock," catering to customers seeking the latest and most popular vape products. Puff Paradise also engages with the community, as evidenced by their calls for support regarding product security and team expansion. Their commitment to customer service and community involvement highlights their role as a trusted local retailer.

Operating from a convenient location at 16383 FM 548, Suite 112, Forney, TX, Puff Paradise maintains extended hours from 9 AM to 10 PM, making it accessible for a broad range of customers. The business also actively recruits motivated and reliable team members, indicating growth and a focus on quality staffing.
